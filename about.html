Our Story

For years I’ve wanted to test my entrepreneurial spirit but could never find the right opportunity. After graduating from the University of Notre Dame with a business degree, I moved to Denver and worked for a few different companies and nonprofits but nothing ever really caught my attention quite like dishing up fresh crêpes. A few months ago I started putting it into action, and the result so far has been nothing short of amazing! It really is a pleasure to cook delicious, fresh food for everyone who stops by our stand. We all want to wish you a very happy day - with many crêpes to fill your stomachs! My wife Abbey and I met the old-fashioned way five years ago - on Tinder. We got engaged in September 2020 and bought a house soon after as the perfect crêpe testing station. We just got married at the end of April and took our honeymoon to Paris and Soissons, France where I got to experience and learn from some incredible crêpe chefs. As many things do with us, what started as a joke became the best part of our lives and the inspiration for our logo. Winston is 2 now and he’s even been promoted to Corgo Crêpe Inspector. You can usually find him wearing his red bandana at our markets, shamelessly asking for a taste test.
